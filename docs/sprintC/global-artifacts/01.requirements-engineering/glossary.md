# Glossary

**Terms, Expressions and Acronyms (TEA) must be organized alphabetically.**


| **_TEA_** (EN)                               | **_Description_** (EN)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |                                       
|:---------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **AC**                                       | Acronym for acceptance criteria.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Agenda**                                   | Where the planning of the tasks from the To-Do List to be completed is done.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **Assembly Point**                           | Assembly Points are locations previously determined and known by all employees, where people must meet in the event of an evacuation or emergency.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Check-Up**                                 | A thorough examination of an vehicle that is needed to do when a certain benchmark of kms is almost met, it also sets the next benchmark for the next check-up, while registering it's kms during the check-up.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Cleaner**                                  | Job of a collaborator that is tasked to clean all sort of places.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Collaborator**                             | A person who is an employee of the organization and carries out design, construction and/or maintenance tasks for green areas, depending on their skills.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Degree of Urgency**                        | Each task has a degree of urgency (High, Medium and Low).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Diary**                                    | The diary is a crucial mechanism for planning the week’s work. Each entry in the diary defines a task (previously on the to-do list) that will be carried out by a team in a green space at a certain time interval on a specific date                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| **Emergency Plan**                           | Managing green spaces for collective use, it is necessary to define an emergency plan that includes Assembly Points and Evacuation Routes.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Equipment**                                | The equipment can be greatly diverse, such as sprayers, lifting platforms, chainsaws, brush cutters, blowers, ladders, cisterns and the various elements that can be attached to tractors, such as disc harrows, weeders, aerators and scarifiers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Evacuation Route**                         | Evacuation Routes indicate the path that users must follow from multiple locations in the park to a Assembly Point from which an organized evacuation of the park will take place.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **File**                                     | A collection of data, programs, stored in a computer's memory under a single identifying name.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Fleet Manager**                            | A person who manages the fleet park, the machines, equipment and vehicles, ensuring their good condition and assigning them to the tasks to be carried out                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Floral Arrangements**                      | Skill of a collaborator that knows how to make floral arrangements.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **FM**                                       | Acronym for Fleet Manager.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Garden**                                   | Garden space with or without trees with little or no equipment.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Garden Tool Maintenance**                  | Skill of a collaborator that knows how to maintenance of the gardening tools.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Gardener**                                 | Job of a collaborator that tends and cultivates plants.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Greenhouse Management**                    | Skill of a collaborator that knows how to manage a green house.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Green Space**                              | The green spaces for collective use managed by MS can significantly vary in size and installed equipment.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Green Spaces Manager**                     | the person responsible for managing the green spaces in charge of the organization                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **GSM**                                      | Acronym for Green Spaces Manager.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Green Spaces User**                        | A person who uses the green spaces managed by the organization and who can through the Portal, make comments or report faults in parks and gardens on the Portal.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **GSU**                                      | Acronym for Green Spaces User.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Human Resources Manager**                  | A person who manages human resources and defines teams based on the needs of ongoing projects and the skills of the employees.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **HRM**                                      | Acronym for Human Resources Manager.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Irrigation System**                        | Irrigation is the artificial application of water to the soil through various systems of tubes, pumps, and sprays.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Java**                                     | Programing language used to develop the application to manage the green space.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Javadoc**                                  | Tool that generates HTML documentation for Java classes, interfaces, methods, fields, and other components of a Java program.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **JavaFX**                                   | Software platform that enables graphical representation of user interface.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Job**                                      | Main occupation of an collaborator                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Jupyter Notebook**                         | Web-based interactive computing platform, used on this project on 5 user stories (US09, US10, US11, US15, US16) related to MATCP.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Key Performance Indicator**                | Indicators like: water or energy consumption, most used pieces of equipment, and park users’ profile by age, for instance.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **KPI**                                      | Acronym for Key Performance Indicator.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| **LAPR2**                                    | Acronym for Laboratory-Project.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Large-sized park**                         | Multi-function space with diverse garden spaces, and woods, including varied equipment and services.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Lawn Care**                                | Skill of collaborator that knows how to lawn care.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **LaTeX**                                    | Software system for typesetting documents.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Machine**                                  | Device used by collaborators, designed to perform tasks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| **Marketing Coordinator**                    | Job of a collaborator that works with management teams to improve marketing efforts by creating and managing advertising campaigns.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **MATCP**                                    | Acronym for Computational Mathematics.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| **MDISC**                                    | Acronym for Discrete Mathematics.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Medium-sized park**                        | Green space with a few hundred or thousands of square meters with a wooded garden area, it includes some infrastructures such as toilets, drinking fountains, irrigation system, lighting, children’s playground.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **MusgoSublime**                             | Organization dedicated to the planning, construction and maintenance of green spaces for collective use in their multiple dimensions                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Nursing Assistant**                        | Job of a collaborator that nurses the plants of the green space.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **OO**                                       | Acronym for Object-Oriented.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **Permission**                               | The action of officially allowing someone to do a particular thing, consent or authorization.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Pest Control and Management**              | Skill of a collaborator that has ability to manage the pests control.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **Plant Identification**                     | Skill of a collaborator that has ability to identify plants.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **PPROG**                                    | Acronym for Programming Paradigms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **President of Sales**                       | Job of a collaborator that is the head of the sales department.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Pruning Techniques**                       | Skill of a collaborator that knows pruning techniques.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| **PVM**                                      | Acronym for Minimal Viable Product.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| **Python**                                   | Programing language used to develop a component of the application that analyses the statistics from the green space.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **QAM**                                      | Acronym for Software Quality Assessment Team Manager.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **Responsibility**                           | The state or fact of having a duty to deal with something or of having control over someone.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| **SCRUM**                                    | Agile framework for team collaboration and problem solving, used in this project.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Seasonal Planting**                        | Skill of a collaborator that knows which plants should plant basing on seasons.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Security**                                 | Job of a collaborator that is responsible to maintain order and help people in need.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Signs to evacuate**                        | Signs that show which routes should be taken in order to evacuate efficiently to the Assembly points.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| **Skill**                                    | The ability of an collaborator to do perform certain tasks.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Statistical Analysis**                     | The analysis of the statistics related to the green space.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Status**                                   | Each task has a status (Planned, Postponed, Canceled and Done).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **Store Employee**                           | Job of a collaborator that works in a store in the green space.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **System User**                              | System that the user has access to in order to interact with the application.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Software Quality Assessment Team Manager** | A person who manages the Software Quality Assessment Team and its process.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| **Soil Analysis**                            | Skill of a collaborator that knows how to analyze soil.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **Task**                                     | Instruction that is given to a collaborator to do.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Task-List**                                | The Task-List comprises all pending tasks for all parks. The entries in this list describe the required task, the degree of urgency (High, Medium, and Low), and the approximate expected duration. The Agenda is made up of entries that relate to a task (which was previously in the To-Do List), the team that will carry out the task, the vehicles/equipment assigned to the task, expected duration, and the status (Planned, Postponed, Canceled, Done).  |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
| **TDD**                                      | Acronym for Test-Driven Development.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **Team**                                     | Team is a temporary association of employees who are assigned to work on a set of tasks in one or more green spaces.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| **To-Do List**                               | The To-Do List comprises all pending tasks for all parks. The entries in this list describe the required task, the degree of urgency (High, Medium, and Low), and the approximate expected duration. The Agenda is made up of entries that relate to a task (which was previously in the To-Do List), the team that will carry out the task, the vehicles/equipment assigned to the task, expected duration, and the status (Planned, Postponed, Canceled, Done). |
| **Tree Care**                                | Skill of a collaborator that knows how to care trees and their maintenance.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **US**                                       | Acronym for user story.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| **User Portal**                              | The green spaces management aims, within the available budget, to design, build and maintain parks and gardens that meet the requirements of the people who want to be informed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Vehicle**                                  | Machine that transports either people or equipment used by collaborators.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **VFM**                                      | Acronym for Vehicle and Equipment Fleet Manager.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Weed Control**                             | Skill of a collaborator that knows what should be done to keep weed out.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
